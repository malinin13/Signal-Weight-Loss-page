Meet Our Founder: Kristin MacDowell

Like most of America, I grew up stuffing my face with fast food. I worked at a pizza joint in high school and then went on to graze the buffet line in college. Before long I didn’t recognize myself in the mirror. The food industry destroys the perfect bodies we are given at birth. I’m sure you’ve heard it the saying “calorie in, calorie out” on the web, from a doctor or personal trainer. All that weight-loss information is based on a flawed and misguided theory. They think you need to work out more and eat less; it’s all about self-control, right? Wrong!

I threw in the towel on weight-loss misinformation and enrolled in Cornell University’s Nutrition Program to learn firsthand from the world’s leading researchers. These are the men and women who help set global food policy. I was shocked by the information. Every day of class my fellow students raised their hands, constantly asking: “Why doesn’t everyone know this?” I’m not one to dwell on conspiracy; I’m here to put forth the real answer. Nobody gets rich off of proper nutrition, but we all know people who have become poor from improper nutrition.

Morbid obesity runs rampant in my family. It’s so severe that it has caused immobility in many of my older relatives. I didn’t want to end up on a scooter in a Missouri farm town dreaming of the Hollywood lifestyle. With The Signal Method you'll learn how to make the right food choices that fly in the face of bogus medical and professional advice. You will become a living example of the truth. And it will practically happen overnight!

Nutrition is the most important aspect of The Signal Method. I learned firsthand from the greatest nutritional and biological scientists at Cornell what it takes for proper health. These are the scientists, such as Dr. Colin Campbell, who advise our federal government and the World Health Organization on human nutrient requirements.

The Signal Method was created to exceed all requirements for every nutrient. There are no supplements required except vitamin B12. The bacteria that creates B12 occurs on the surface of our food. Humans no longer consume this vitamin because we wash it off during food preparation. B12 occurs in the soil and our gut. Wild animals receive B12 because they don’t wash their dinner before eating it. Humans have to sacrifice a little B12 when we wash off all the other muck!

In the end, it’s all about the nutrients. Feed your body right for the best results. Once you realize how easy it is, there’s no turning back. Soon your friends will ask you what's your secret. Just smile and say it's really that simple!
